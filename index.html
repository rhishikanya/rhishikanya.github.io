About

I am a Research Scholar in the Department of Assamese at Tezpur University, Guwahati. My research primarily focuses on exploring the role of semiotics in film studies, with an emphasis on analyzing symbolic representations, cultural narratives, and visual storytelling in Assamese cinema. In addition to academic and research pursuits, I am also actively involved in creative literature. My first book, Rathi (a collection of short stories), was published in 2022, followed by my novel Rudrani in 2024. Beyond writing, I am passionate about debate, poetry recitation, and public speaking, which reflect my enthusiasm for engaging with diverse forms of expression and communication.
