Projects & Related Publications

My area of research broadly includes semiotic analysis of Assamese cinema employing advanced digital methodologies, as well as computational linguistics. Some of my ongoing research works are listed below:

Character Networks in Assamese Cinema

Ongoing

Title: Character Networks in Assamese Cinema

The study of character networks provides a systematic approach towards an understanding of the dynamics and relations that drive plots and hence derives considerable insight into the core elements forming the fabric of narration. The work focuses on a thorough exploration of the character network in Assamese films.The primary goal is to visualize narrative dynamics and uncover how character relationships evolve over time.

Related Publications:

  • R. Batswa, P. Borah, S. J. Neog, “Exploring Character Networks in Assamese Cinema: A Graph-Based Analysis,” 2025 International Conference on Innovation in Computing and Engineering (IEEE ICE 2025).
